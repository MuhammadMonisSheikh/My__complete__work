The fourth paragraph does not go across to the left hand edge of the page as one might expect. Rather it sits right under the third paragraph. The reason for this is that the fourth paragraph has space to start under the third paragraph, but it cannot go any further to the left because the second paragraph is in the way. Setting the height of the paragraphs to be the same height as the tallest paragraph would solve this issue, but it is rarely suited to real world designs where the amount of text in a paragraph or column may vary. It is more common to use the clear property (discussed on the next page) to solve this issue.
